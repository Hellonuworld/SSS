Packaging

Export packing that protects the surface.

Proper stainless steel packaging helps prevent transit damage, corrosion and cross-contamination from rust, dust or other impurities. We use export-focused packing methods based on the product form and destination.

Wooden Pallets & Boxes

Coils, sheets and strips can be packed on wooden pallets or in wooden boxes for safer long-distance transport and container loading.

Paper, Frames & Straps

Partition paper, protective frames and fastening straps are added when needed to reduce surface scratches and movement during shipment.

Clean Material Handling

Packing materials are selected to avoid contaminating stainless steel surfaces, helping customers receive clean and usable material.
